    Poster making & Doodling

    "Art speaks when words are unable to explain. After all, creativity is the greatest rebellion in existence."

    Who said you could only write a paper to present your ideas to the world? You could also splash some colours on a paper and make it speak a thousand words!Â This January, come, let your creativity explode!

    General Rules:

    1. No. of participant is one per team.
    2. Topic will be disclosed at the venue.
    3. Artwork may include written slogans, graphics and artwork.
    4. Artwork may NOT include any official trademarked logos.
    5. Decision of the judges will be final.
    6. All artwork will be the sole property of the organisers after the competition is over.
    7. They cannot contain any content, element, or material that violates a third party's publicity, privacy or intellectual property rights.

    Poster Making:

    1. Participants will have to bring their own art material.
    2. The materials may include:
      1. Paints (Poster colours or acrylic colours or water colours allowed)
      2. Drawing board and drawing pins
      3. Pencil and Eraser
      4. Paint Brushes
      5. Use of scale and compass is allowed.
    3. Participants will be provided with 1/2 a chart paper for poster making by the organizers.
    4. Time limit : 1.5 hours

    Doodling:

    1. Participants will have to bring their own art material.
    2. Usage of any kind of pens, pencils, markers are allowed as long as it is free hand.
    3. Participants will be provided with A4 size drawing sheet for doodling by the organizers.
    4. Time limit : 1 hour.
