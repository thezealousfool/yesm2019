    • Shutter Up

    Photography

    Showcase your skills in photojournalism and preserve moments that otherwise would have been forgotten.

    Rules:

    1. Teams of 1 or 2 member(s) are allowed.
    2. Cross-college teams are allowed.
    3. School students (11 & 12) are allowed to participate but no cross School- college team is allowed.
    4. Participants are to cover the events of YESM 2019 and will be awarded on the basis of their skills in photojournalism.
    5. Slots will be given to each team at their convenience.
    6. Participants must arrive on venue each day before the events start.

    Submission Details:

    1. At the end of each day, each team are to submit a number of photographs (number to be specified for each day). They will be judged on the basis of these submitted photographs.
    2. All photographs clicked are to be mailed to submissions@yesm.in with the subject “Shutter Up - Photography". Name(s) of registrant(s) should be clearly mentioned in the mail along with institution name(s).

    Process of Selection:

    Judge's Choice Award: All submissions made will be viewed by the judge. Marking is at his/her discretion.
