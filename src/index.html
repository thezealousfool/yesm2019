    16th, 17th and 18th January, 2019

    About YESM

    The Young Economist Students’ Meet is an initiative pioneered by the students of the Department of Economics, Jadavpur University. The meet features a number of exciting competitive events for school and college students to participate in, along with guest lectures and panel discussions, by eminent scholars and academicians. At YESM, students explore uncharted territories- both creative and academic, in the study and application of Economics.

    Theme

    The theme for YESM 2019 is ‘A Decade After Financial Crisis’. During the two years 2007-08, the world witnessed an economic calamity likes of which had never been seen before. A dramatic collapse of the global financial markets sent the world economy reeling. Now with the passage of decade, it is now time for us to look back upon those turbulent years to assess what went wrong and what should be done to prevent its recurrence in future. YESM 2019 aims to do just that.

    Department of Economics

    The Department of Economics, Jadavpur University, is recognised nationwide for its excellence in teaching and prospective research activities. Prof. Amartya Sen, the noted Welfare Economist and Nobel Laureate began his career here, as a teacher and a research scholar. Established in the year 1956, the department has consistently impressed the University Grants Commission with its teaching and research activities in various fields of economics. In 2005, the Department became the 4th Centre of Advanced Studies (CAS) in Economics in India.

    Previous Editions

    At YESM, we aim to discuss economic issues that are interesting and pertinent at the moment to India and the world. Since its inception in 2007, YESM has seen discussions on a number of diverse and challenging topics, and has hosted many distinguished figures from both academia and industry.
